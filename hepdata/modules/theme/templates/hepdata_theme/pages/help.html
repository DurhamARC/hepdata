Submission Steps

Submissions are handled by coordinators for a paper or project. These coordinators assign uploaders and reviewers to each submission.

Emails are sent to the assigned uploaders and reviewers with a link which when accessed gives the user the privileges to either upload or review a data submission.

All past and present submissions are available for a coordinator, reviewer, or uploader to see in the dashboard.

Once the data is all uploaded and reviewed, the coordinator can make a final check on the submission to ensure its correctness, and the entire submission will be published and searchable in HEPData.

Advice for Reviewers


The HEPData review system is completely self contained. You can have discussions about each data table and its associated plot, as well as approve or reject a plot directly from the interface.


The data tables have interactive plots automatically generated given particular data types, so you can also visually query the data to find mistakes in the data.
